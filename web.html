关于我们 About Us

綦江精锐达重型齿轮有限公司创建于1988年，公司坐落于重庆市綦江区桥河工业园区西齿大道3号，紧临渝黔高速公路古剑山出口，距重庆主城区60余公里，交通便捷。公司占地面积68亩，配备各类专业先进生产设备130余台（套），员工100余人，其中工程技术人员10人，是一家专业生产、销售汽车、摩托车、叉车齿轮加工零部件的企业。

Qijiang Jingruida Heavy Gear Co., Ltd., founded in 1988, is located at No.3, West Gear Avenue, Qiaohe Industrial Park, Qijiang District, Chongqing. Close to the Gujian Mountain exit of the Chongqing–Guizhou Expressway, it is about 60 kilometers from Chongqing’s main urban area with convenient transportation. Jingruida covers an area of 68 acres, equipped with over 130 pieces of advanced professional production equipment and more than 100 employees, including 10 engineering and technical staff. The company is a professional component manufacturer producing and selling gears for automobiles, motorcycles, and forklifts.

公司一直以来十分重视技术创新，成立重庆市中小企业研发中心一个，历年来多次获得国家知识产权局颁发专利证书，拥有8项实用新型专利；并于2018年被重庆市科技局评为“高新技术企业”，2019年被评为“专精特新”企业。

Jingruida has always attached great importance to technological innovation and has established a research and development center for small and medium-sized enterprises in Chongqing. Over the years, the company has obtained professional certificates issued by the China National Intellectual Property Administration many times, with 8 utility model patents. In 2018, it was rated a “high‑tech enterprise” by the Chongqing Science and Technology Bureau, and in 2019 it was rated a “specialized, refined, and innovative enterprise.”

近年来，公司主要为重汽(济南)桥箱有限公司配套轮边齿轮，以及各类轴套、齿套、主动和从动齿轮等产品；为西安卓博液压工程有限公司生产特种车辆齿轮；为浙江中柴机器股份有限公司生产叉车齿轮；为綦江齿轮传动有限公司生产客车及货车变速箱齿轮。多次获得国内知名主机厂优秀供应商称号。

In recent years, Jingruida mainly produces wheel‑side bridge gears as well as various shift sleeves, driving and driven gears and other products for Heavy Duty Truck (Jinan) Bridge Box Co., Ltd. It also produces special‑vehicle gears for Xi’an Zhuoshi Hydraulic Engineering Co., Ltd., forklift gears for Zhejiang Zhongchai Machinery Co., Ltd., and passenger and freight car gearbox gears for Qijiang Gear Transmission Co., Ltd. Jingruida has been awarded the title of “domestically well‑known and excellent component supplier of OEMs” multiple times.

公司视产品质量为企业生命，顺利通过IATF16949质量第三方认证；在此基础上继续完善生产、质量管理体系。我公司以良好的产品质量和服务赢得了各个主机厂客户的信任，成为他们的战略伙伴企业和定点配套企业。

We regard product quality as our lifeline and have successfully passed the third‑party certification of the IATF16949 quality system. On this basis, we continue to improve our production and quality management systems. With excellent product quality and service, we have won the trust of customers from various OEMs and become their strategic partner and designated supporting enterprise.
